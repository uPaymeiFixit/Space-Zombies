Space Zombies

Launch project
This is a game that takes advantage of HTML5's canvas element. I wrote this draft of the game to help my friend get started with one of his games. This is one of the games I use to test different multiplayer configurations using Socket.io and Node.js. The player is the white dot that is in the center of the screen, and can move around moving the mouse. The object of the game is to avoid touching any of the zombie-like enemy. The player can hold down the mouse button to shoot and kill enemy.
